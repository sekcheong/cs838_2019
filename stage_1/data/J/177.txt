Actor <name>Gerard Jugnot</name> - star of the Oscar-nominated film The Chorus - has beaten <name>Gerard Depardieu</name> to become France's best-paid actor of 2004.

The Chorus, which <name>Jugnot</name> also co-produced, has drawn an audience of nearly nine million people since its release last year.

She starred in <name>Jean-Pierre Jeunet</name>'s A Very Long Engagement (Un Long Dimanche de Fiancailles) and is also lined up to co-star with Tom Hanks in <name>Ron Howard</name>'s The <name>Da Vinci Code</name>. The Chorus is nominated for best foreign film at Sunday's Oscar ceremony. On Saturday, it will compete for the title of best film against fellow nominee A Very Long Engagement in France's Cesar film awards.
